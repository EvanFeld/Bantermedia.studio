Got Questions?

FREQUENTLY
ASKED

The free audit, the offers, and how we work. Still curious? Ask us directly — it's free.

What exactly is the Free Website Audit?

Two things, both free: a straight-talking teardown of your current site (what's costing you customers and why), and a mockup of your brand refreshed so you can see the new look before you spend a dollar. Request it on the Free Audit page — no commitment, no sales pressure.

Do I have to rebrand or get a new logo?

No — that's the whole point. We build on the brand you already have: same logo, same name, same colors. We put it where it belongs — sharp and consistent everywhere it shows up. If you want tweaks we can talk, but a rebrand is never required.

What are the offers?

Four ways in: Websites (new build or full redesign), Branding Kits (your logo on cards, apparel, mugs, and signage — shown on real mockups first), Launch Kits (branded Instagram ads and launch creatives), and Content (photo, video, and reels). The flagship is The Full Refresh — site + branding + launch in one rollout, so everything matches on day one.

How much does it cost?

Website builds run from $900 (The Launchpad) up to $8,000–12K+ (Custom Pro), with our most popular Growth build at $4,500–7,000. Branding Kits start from $750, Launch Kits from $500, and Content from $250. The full menu is at /pricing — and you still get one fixed quote after the free audit, so there are no surprises.

I'm brand-new — no site, barely a logo. Can you still help?

Yes — The Launchpad ($900–1,500) is built exactly for that: 1–3 pages, mobile-ready, domain and hosting handled, and Google-able from day one. If the logo needs work too, the Branding Kit (from $750) rounds it out. Details on both are at /pricing.

How long does a website take?

Scope decides it, but think weeks, not months — a typical small-business site doesn't need to drag on. You'll get a timeline with your quote, and we don't disappear mid-build.

What happens after launch?

Our maintenance plans — $50/mo Basic or $125/mo Premium — keep the site updated, backed up, and running. No plan is required, but most clients take one so they never have to think about their site again.

Do you still do photo and video?

Absolutely — that's our Content offer, and it's where we started. Sports, live events, weddings, restaurants, grad photos, portraits, recruitment content, reels — all still here. Standard turnaround is 5–7 business days for photos and 7–10 for video edits; rush delivery is available if you ask.

Can we use the photos and videos on social media?

Yes — all delivered content is yours to post. We may also share shots on our portfolio and social pages (with credit). If you need exclusive rights, let us know and we can discuss that separately.

Where do you work?

We're local to The Woodlands, Conroe, Montgomery, Katy, Magnolia, and the greater Houston area. Websites, branding kits, and launch kits can happen from anywhere; shoots are local unless we arrange travel — fees may apply depending on distance, and we're upfront about that when we talk.

What's the deposit and cancellation policy for shoots?

We require a deposit to lock in your date. Cancellations made more than 72 hours before the event are eligible for a partial refund. We'll walk you through everything when we confirm your booking.
