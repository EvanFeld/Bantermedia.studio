Got Questions?

FREQUENTLY
ASKED

The free audit, the offers, and how we work. Still curious? Ask us directly — it's free.

What exactly is the Free Facelift Audit?

Two things, both free: a straight-talking teardown of your current site (what's costing you customers and why), and a mockup of your brand refreshed so you can see the new look before you spend a dollar. Request it on the Free Audit page — no commitment, no sales pressure.

Do I have to rebrand or get a new logo?

No — that's the whole point. We build on the brand you already have: same logo, same name, same colors. We make it look expensive and consistent everywhere it shows up. If you want tweaks we can talk, but a rebrand is never required.

What are the offers?

Four ways in: The Website (new build or full redesign), The Branding Kit (your logo on cards, apparel, mugs, and signage — shown on real mockups first), The Launch Kit (branded Instagram ads and launch creatives), and Content (photo, video, and reels). The flagship is The Full Facelift — site + branding + launch in one rollout, so everything matches on day one.

How much does it cost?

Depends on what you take — one offer or the whole facelift. You'll get a clear quote after the free audit, so you know exactly what you're saying yes to before anything starts. The audit itself costs nothing either way.

How long does a website take?

Scope decides it, but think weeks, not months — a typical small-business site doesn't need to drag on. You'll get a timeline with your quote, and we don't disappear mid-build.

Do you still do photo and video?

Absolutely — that's our Content offer, and it's where we started. Sports, live events, weddings, restaurants, grad photos, portraits, recruitment content, reels — all still here. Standard turnaround is 5–7 business days for photos and 7–10 for video edits; rush delivery is available if you ask.

Can we use the photos and videos on social media?

Yes — all delivered content is yours to post. We may also share shots on our portfolio and social pages (with credit). If you need exclusive rights, let us know and we can discuss that separately.

Where do you work?

We're local to Magnolia, College Station, and the greater Houston area. Websites, branding kits, and launch kits can happen from anywhere; shoots are local unless we arrange travel — fees may apply depending on distance, and we're upfront about that when we talk.

What's the deposit and cancellation policy for shoots?

We require a deposit to lock in your date. Cancellations made more than 72 hours before the event are eligible for a partial refund. We'll walk you through everything when we confirm your booking.
