The Brief

A REAL COMPANY, REIMAGINED

The Business

Roofs, metal, and barndominiums

Exteriors of Texas builds roofing, metal buildings, and barndominiums across Magnolia and the greater Northwest Houston area — a real local company with real reputation to show for it.

The Problem

A dated template site

Their current site leans on a generic builder template — a low trust signal for a trades company whose whole pitch is doing the job right. The quality of the work doesn't come through online.

What a Prototype Shows

Direction, not a finished system

This mock proves a direction — the layout, the tone, the feel of a homepage built from scratch. It's a starting point, on purpose: not a full brand system, not a delivered build.

The Concept

A HOMEPAGE, FROM A BLANK PAGE
