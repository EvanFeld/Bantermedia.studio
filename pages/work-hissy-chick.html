The Brief

A LOUD BRAND, A QUIET SITE

The Business

Nashville hot, Texan warm

Hissy Chick makes each Nashville-style sandwich to order — Chick Sauce, Creamy Serrano, Italian sweet sourdough. A food truck and a storefront on FM 1488, a loyal Magnolia following, and a voice with real swagger.

The Problem

The site didn't say a word

The old page was flat and forgettable — no personality, no clear way to order, none of the noise that makes Hissy Chick fun in person. Great food hiding behind a bland front door.

What We Built

Turned the volume up

A fast, mobile-first site that talks like they do, points straight to online ordering via Square, and rides on a full brand system — color, type, wordmark, components — carried through a 6-piece ad set.

The Website

LIVE, LOUD, AND SELLING

The real site at hissychick.net — every screen built to do one thing: get you a sandwich. Menu up front, online ordering one tap away, and the brand voice on every line.
