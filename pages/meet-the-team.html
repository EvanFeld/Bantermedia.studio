Our Story

Banter Media Group was born out of a love for the college experience — the energy, the brotherhood, the moments you want to last forever. We started with a camera, and everywhere we shot we kept seeing the same thing: great local businesses stuck with dated websites and a look that didn't match who they were. So we became the fix. Today we give small businesses the full facelift — a modern site and a matching look across everything — built with the same eye we've always shot with, on the brand you already have.
