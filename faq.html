ASKED

Everything you need to know before booking. Still have questions? Hit us on the Interested page.

How do I book Banter Media Group?

Head to our Interested page, fill out the form with your event details, and we'll get back to you within 48 hours to confirm availability and talk through the details.

What types of events do you cover?

We shoot sports, weddings, restaurants, events & frat parties, and grad photos. If you're not sure whether your event fits, reach out anyway — chances are we can make it work.

How long until we receive our photos or video?

Standard turnaround is 5–7 business days for photos and 7–10 business days for video edits, depending on length of video. Rush delivery is available — just mention it when you reach out and we'll discuss options.

Do you travel for events?

Yes. We're based in Houston, TX. But we will travel for the right event. Travel fees may apply depending on distance — we'll be upfront about that when we talk.

Do you offer video in addition to photos?

Absolutely. We do full event recaps, highlight reels, and short-form social-ready content. You can book photo only, video only, or both — just specify in your inquiry.

How many edited photos will we receive?

The number depends on the length of the event and the package you choose. We'll give you a clear expected count upfront. We never deliver rushed or low-quality edits just to hit a number.

Can we use the photos and videos on social media?

Yes — all delivered content is yours to post. We may also share shots on our portfolio and social pages (with credit). If you need exclusive rights, let us know and we can discuss that separately.

What's the deposit and cancellation policy?

We require a deposit to lock in your date. Cancellations made more than 72 hours before the event are eligible for a partial refund. We'll walk you through everything when we confirm your booking.
